U.S. Bank Failures

Locate and research the more-than-375 U.S. banking companies have been shuttered by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation since Oct. 1 2000.

In viewing the FDIC's online list, no distinction was made between banking companies operating a single branch or multiple branches in multiple states.

To view banking companies that failed prior to Oct. 1, 2000, the FDIC offers this search.
