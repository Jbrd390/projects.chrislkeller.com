On April 5, 2011, Madison voters choose former Mayor Paul Soglin over two-term incumbent Mayor Dave Cieslewicz, 9.77 percent to 48.97 percent. Click on the map to find voting totals for each Madison ward. About 1,133 write-in votes are not reflected on this map.
